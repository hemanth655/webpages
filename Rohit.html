ROHIT SHARMA

Rohit Gurunath Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian international Cricketer who currently captains the India national cricket team team across all formats. He is a right-handed batsman. Considered one of the best batsmen of his generation and one of the greatest opening batters of all time,[4] Sharma is known for his timing, elegance, six-hitting abilities and leadership skills. Sharma holds several batting records which famously include most sixes in international cricket,[a] most double centuries in ODI cricket (3), most centuries at Cricket World Cups (7) and joint most hundreds in Twenty20 Internationals (5). Rohit Sharma is the first player to score 5 T20I centuries.[6] He plays for Mumbai Indians in ILP and for Mumbai in domestic cricket.
